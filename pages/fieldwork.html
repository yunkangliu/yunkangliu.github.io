Fieldwork

Thailand and Laos Energy Transition Research

September – December 2025

Fieldwork conducted in Thailand and Laos focusing on energy transition, electric vehicle (EV) industrial policy, and renewable energy development.

  • Interviews with EV companies in Thailand including MG (SAIC–CP) and Great Wall Motors.
  • Consultations with Thai government agencies including the Eastern Economic Corridor (EEC), Industrial Estate Authority of Thailand, Thailand EV Association, Ministry of Energy, and Ministry of Industry.
  • Site visits to industrial zones and ports in the Eastern Economic Corridor.
  • Field research in Laos on renewable energy projects including hydropower, solar, wind, and power grid development.
  • Interviews with companies such as China Power and China General Nuclear Power, as well as Lao government officials and academic researchers.

Inclusive Business and Social Enterprise Research

July 2020 – Nakhon Pathom Province, Thailand

Field research at Suan Sampran social enterprise examining inclusive business models and sustainable livelihoods among small-scale farmers.

  • Interviews with project managers and participating farmers.
  • Research on the role of social enterprises in supporting low-income communities.

Cross-border Development in Southern Thailand and Northern Malaysia

2020 – Hat Yai, Pattani (Thailand) and Northern Peninsular Malaysia

Field research examining cross-border economic relations, local development, and regional identity in Southern Thailand and Northern Malaysia.

  • Visits to Hat Yai Municipality and Songkhla Community Foundation.
  • Research on rural and urban development in Songkhla and Pattani provinces.
  • Analysis of cross-border trade, tourism, and historical relations between Thailand and Malaysia.
  • Consideration of regional conflict dynamics in Southern Thailand.
