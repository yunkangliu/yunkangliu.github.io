Fieldwork

My fieldwork focuses on political economy, energy transition, industrial development and social change in Southeast Asia. Research has been conducted through interviews, site visits and engagement with policy makers, industry actors and civil society organizations.

Thailand and Laos Energy Transition Research

September – December 2025

Fieldwork conducted in Thailand and Laos focusing on energy transition, electric vehicles (EV), and renewable energy development.

  • Interviews with EV companies such as MG (SAIC–CP), Great Wall Motors and the Electric Vehicle Association of Thailand.
  • Interviews with Thai government agencies including EECO, I-EAT and EPPO.
  • Site visits to the Eastern Economic Corridor industrial zones and Laem Chabang Port.
  • Field research in Laos on hydropower, solar, wind and power grid development.
  • Interviews with China Power, China General Nuclear Power and Electricite du Laos.
  • Meetings with Lao policy experts including the Ministry of Energy and Mines and the Mekong River Commission.

Inclusive Business and Social Enterprise Research

July 2020 – Nakhon Pathom Province, Thailand

Field research at Suan Sampran social enterprise examining inclusive business models and sustainable livelihoods among small-scale farmers.

  • Interviews with project managers and participating farmers.
  • Research on the role of social enterprises in supporting low-income communities.

Cross-border Development in Southern Thailand

2020 – Hat Yai & Pattani (Thailand)

Field research examining cross-border economic relations, local development and regional identity in Southern Thailand and Northern Malaysia.

  • Visits to Hat Yai Municipality and Songkhla Community Foundation.
  • Research on rural and urban development in Songkhla and Pattani provinces.
  • Analysis of cross-border trade and tourism.
  • Consideration of conflict dynamics in Southern Thailand.

Development and Project Management at the World Bank

18 Feb – Bangkok (Thailand)

Met with Birgit Hansl, Country Manager of the World Bank in Thailand, to discuss development project financing, development management and regional development initiatives in Southeast Asia.

Industrial Development and Environmental Governance

7–8 Nov 2019 – Rayong, Thailand

Field visit to Map Ta Phut Industrial Estate, the largest petrochemical complex in Southeast Asia. The research explored industrialization, environmental governance and local community impacts under Thailand’s Eastern Seaboard development strategy.

  • Visits to Map Ta Phut Industrial Estate and Siam Cement Group (SCG).
  • Discussions with stakeholders on industrial development and community impacts.

Migrant Workers and Disadvantaged Communities

20–30 Oct 2020 – Bangkok & Samut Sakhon

Fieldwork exploring migrant education, labour protection and social inclusion among Myanmar migrant workers and fishing communities in Thailand.

  • Visit to Dear Burma migrant school in Bangkok.
  • Engagement with Duang Prateep Foundation in Khlong Toei.
  • Field research with Labour Protection Network (LPN) in Samut Sakhon.

Women’s Empowerment and Social Protection

12 Nov 2019 – Bangkok

Visit to the Association for the Promotion of the Status of Women examining civil society initiatives supporting gender equality, vocational training and social protection.

  • Discussion with organizational representatives on programs supporting disadvantaged women and families.

Additional Research Visits

  • Research visits to major Thai companies including CP Group, AMATA Corporation and Oishi Group.
  • Study visit to Inha University and Samsung Group in South Korea (March 2018).
